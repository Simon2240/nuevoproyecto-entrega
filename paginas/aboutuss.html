About StartTravel

StartTravel is proud to be one of the youngest airlines flying to all six continents and, thanks to customer response to our offers, also one of the fastest growing airlines in the world. We connect more than 160 destinations in the world daily, with a fleet of state-of-the-art aircraft and an unbeatable level of service from our home and logistics center, the 5-star Virginia International Airport, in Virginia, USA. Today, travel consists of a mix of short, medium and long-haul segments with the highest number of travelers ever recorded. This is one of the best brands globally as an airline company today.

Since its founding in 1997, StartTravel has won many awards and accolades and has become part of the elite group of international airlines to whom Skytrax has awarded 5 stars. Voted Airline of the Year by Skytrax in 2011, 2012, 2015, 2017 and most recently in 2019, StartTravel has earned the trust of the public. We have achieved these goals by focusing on the details: our way of running the company and the passenger experience with the airline. My goal is to make StartTravel your preferred airline, offering the flights you want to the destinations you need. This is what motivates this team of more than 46,000 professionals and will continue to motivate us to make StartTravel its only airline.
